# Roles & Responsibilities Matrix (Waterfall)

These role definitions provide a baseline for Waterfall governance and accountability. Tailor the
roles, decision rights, and escalation paths to match your organizational structure, and add any
specialized roles required by regulators or line-of-business practices.

| Role | Description | Key Responsibilities | Decision Authority | Escalation Path |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Project Sponsor | Executive accountable for outcomes, funding, and strategic alignment. | Approves charter, secures funding, resolves cross-functional conflicts, provides direction on benefits realization. | Approve funding, scope changes above PM authority, and major trade-offs. | Escalate to steering committee or executive leadership. |
| Project Manager | Leads end-to-end delivery with focus on schedule, scope, cost, and risk. | Builds plans and baselines, manages budget and vendors, coordinates resources, reports status, enforces change control. | Approve day-to-day delivery decisions within delegated authority. | Escalate major risks, issues, or scope changes to Sponsor/CCB. |
| Business Analyst | Translates business needs into requirements and acceptance criteria. | Elicits requirements, documents functional specs, defines acceptance criteria, supports UAT and traceability. | Approve requirement baselines and clarifications. | Escalate requirements conflicts to PM and Sponsor. |
| Functional Lead / Team Lead | Oversees delivery within a functional domain (e.g., engineering, operations). | Plans and assigns work, ensures technical quality, manages dependencies, reports progress and blockers. | Approve technical decisions within domain. | Escalate resource or design conflicts to PM. |
| Quality Manager | Ensures quality standards, compliance, and audit readiness. | Defines QA/QC plan, conducts reviews, tracks defects, verifies acceptance criteria, manages audits. | Accept/reject deliverables against quality standards. | Escalate quality risks to PM and Sponsor. |
| Change Control Board (CCB) | Governance body for scope, schedule, and cost changes. | Reviews change requests, evaluates impacts, approves or rejects changes, updates baselines. | Approve/reject change requests beyond PM authority. | Escalate unresolved changes to Sponsor. |
| Project Team Member | Individual contributor delivering assigned work products. | Executes tasks, provides estimates, reports progress, identifies risks, participates in reviews. | Decide task-level execution approach within assigned scope. | Escalate blockers to Functional Lead/PM. |

## Usage Notes
- Align accountability with the project charter and RACI assignments.
- Capture decision thresholds (budget, timeline, scope) in the governance plan.

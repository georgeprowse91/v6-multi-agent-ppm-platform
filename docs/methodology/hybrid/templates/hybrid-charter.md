# Hybrid Project Charter

## Purpose
Blend upfront planning with iterative delivery by defining objectives, governance cadence, and
delivery approach in a single charter.

## Audience
Program Sponsor, Program Manager, Delivery Lead, PMO Lead, Product Owner.

## Inputs
- Business case (`docs/templates/shared/business-case.md`).
- Demand intake (`docs/templates/shared/demand-intake-request.md`).
- Governance standards and compliance requirements.

## Definitions
- **Stage gate:** Formal approval checkpoint before progressing to the next phase.
- **Iteration:** Time-boxed delivery cycle within a stage.

## Methodology Gate Alignment
- Hybrid Initiation Gate (`docs/methodology/hybrid/gates.yaml`).

## Required Sections
- Project overview
- Objectives & outcomes
- Governance cadence
- Scope & constraints
- Delivery plan
- Risk & dependency highlights
- Approvals

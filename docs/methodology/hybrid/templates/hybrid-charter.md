## 16. Adaptation Guide
### When to Emphasize Traditional Elements
- Regulatory compliance or fixed scope/timeline requirements.
- Formal documentation and audit readiness expectations.

### When to Emphasize Agile Elements
- Rapidly evolving requirements and customer feedback loops.
- Innovation-driven outcomes and uncertainty.

### Balancing Guidance
1. Segment the project by risk and certainty.
2. Define integration points between stage gates and iterations.
3. Align reporting cadence across both delivery modes.
4. Track both predictive and agile metrics.
5. Right-size documentation to risk and complexity.

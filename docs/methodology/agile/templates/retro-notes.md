# Sprint Retrospective Notes

## Purpose
Capture lessons learned, identify improvements, and create actionable commitments for the next
sprint.

## Audience
Scrum Master, Delivery Team, Product Owner, Stakeholders (as invited).

## Inputs
- Sprint outcomes and metrics.
- Burndown or delivery tracking (`docs/methodology/agile/templates/burndown.xlsx`).
- Feedback from review or stakeholders.

## Definitions
- **Improvement action:** A specific, owned change to implement in the next sprint.
- **Experiment:** A time-boxed change to test and evaluate.

## Methodology Gate Alignment
- Agile gate: Retrospective Close Gate (`docs/methodology/agile/gates.yaml`).

## Required Sections
- Sprint summary
- What went well
- What did not go well
- Opportunities / experiments
- Action items
- Appreciations
- Approval and follow-up plan

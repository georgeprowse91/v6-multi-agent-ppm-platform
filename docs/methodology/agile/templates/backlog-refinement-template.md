# Backlog Refinement Template

## Purpose
Structure backlog refinement sessions to improve story quality, estimate work, and resolve
dependencies before sprint planning.

## When to Use
- Use during backlog refinement sessions to capture decisions, estimates, and open questions.
- Use to prepare stories for Definition of Ready before sprint planning.
- Use alongside (not instead of) backlog management summaries for overall health tracking.

## Audience
Product Owner, Scrum Master, delivery team, and relevant stakeholders.

## Inputs
- Product backlog and roadmap priorities.
- Definition of Ready and Definition of Done.
- Dependency and risk logs.

# Sprint Review Template

## Purpose
Document sprint review preparation and outcomes so stakeholders see demonstrated value, feedback
is captured, and backlog implications are recorded.

## Audience
Scrum Master, Product Owner, Delivery Team, Stakeholders.

## Inputs
- Sprint plan (`docs/methodology/agile/templates/sprint-plan.md`).
- Sprint metrics and delivery reports.
- Demo environment readiness checks.

## Methodology Gate Alignment
- Agile gate: Sprint Review Gate (`docs/methodology/agile/gates.yaml`).

## Required Sections
- Meeting logistics and agenda
- Sprint goal achievement and metrics
- Demo scenarios and results
- Stakeholder feedback
- Backlog implications and next steps
- Action items and decisions
